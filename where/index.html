Where is Dokdo?

Location of Dokdo


Administrative Position and The latitude and longitude of the Dokdo

   Administrative Position of the Dokdo

  •   Dokdo is in '1~96 Dokdo-ri, Ulleung-eup, Ulleung-gun, Gyeongsangbuk-do' Korea Territory.
  •   This beautiful Dokdo is about 87km away from Ulleungdo, so if the weather is clear, you can see Dokdo from Ulleungdo.

   The latitude and longitude of Dokdo

  • Dokdo is betweend Longitude  131°51'~131°53', Latitude 37°14'00"~37°14'45"
  • Dokdo's end of east, Chut-Sum(첫섬) is in longitude 131° 87', Latitude  37° 23'. It is in the east of Korea.
