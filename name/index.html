The Name of Dokdo

We call Dokdo variously.

Original name of Dokdo

The original name for Dokdo was an Usan(우산). Silla Dynasty times, The Isabu(이사부) has conquered the country of Usan(우산국).

It says

『Ulleung(Ulleungdo)』and 『Usan(Dokdo)』are all Usan Country's territory

In "Dongguk Literature Commentary(Dongguk Munheon bigo)"(동국문헌비고)...

A variety of names of the Dokdo.

Dokdo has many names.
Now Let's take a look at the various names one by one.

Usan(우산)

As I explained at first, Usan means Dokdo. The name "Dokdo" is also expressed as "Usan" in "Sejong Sillok Jiriji"(세종실록지리지) and "Dongguk Literature Commentary(Dongguk Munheon bigo yeojigo)"(동국문헌비고 여지고).

Sambong-Do(삼봉도)

Dokdo's old name, Dokdo is the name that was shown with three peaks. That mean is literally an island with three peaks.

Seok-Do(석도)

Kojong changed Ulleungdo to Ulleung by issuing the "Order No. 41(Chikryeong 41ho)"(칙령 41호).The jurisdiction of Ulleungdo was defined as Jukdo and Seokdo by law Article 2.
When we say Seokdo in Korean, it becomes Dol-Seum(Rock Island). The pronunciation of the Dol changed to Dok. So Dokdo until now, is being called 「Dokdo」.

Officials wrote down local names in Chinese characters, which were also 'Seok-Do'. But Japan claims that the island written here is a different island from Dokdo.
However, in the "Josun language Dictionary"(조선어사전) published in 1838, "Dok" is written as a dialect of Seom(Stone).  So We can tell that Seok-Do=Dokdo.

Liancourt Rocks

Liancourt Rocks is a misnomer! You MUST say Dokdo.

The French first arrive at Dokdo and named it Liancourt Rocks, because their boat name is Liancourt. To date, this name is called Liancourt Rocks by other countries such as the US.

There was no one at the time of the discovery, so we called it a reef.
But this is wrong. Before French person come to Dokdo, Many people lived in Dokdo., It is wrong to name Dokdo as Liancourt.
